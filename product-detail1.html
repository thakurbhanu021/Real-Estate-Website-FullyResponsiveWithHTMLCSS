About Saya Residency

Situated at a prime location of Noida (Sector 142), Saya Residency is a meticulously designed project of Noida. Get good quality
yet affordable options in Saya Residency in the price range of Rs. 89. 5 Lac - 1. 97 Cr. The project is spread over a wide area of
940 Sq-m. There are total 76 units in the project that are designed keeping the modern lifestyle in mind.

It is a beautifully constructed Residential property that is sure to please you. The units are all Under Construction. The project
offers property options such as Flat which are unparalleled and value for money. The property units are spacious and range in size
from 1 BHK Flat (550. 0 Sq. Ft. - 550. 0 Sq. Ft. ), 2 BHK Flat (722. 0 Sq. Ft. - 800. 0 Sq. Ft. ), 3 BHK Flat (1000. 0 Sq. Ft. -
1000. 0 Sq. Ft. ).The design of the project is such that it is classified into 1 towers. This premium residential project has a launch
date of 01 June 2022.The estimated possession date of the project is 01 March 2025.

This world class Saya Residency has been granted. its commencement certificate. The status of the occupancy certificate of this project
not granted. Known for their quality constructions Saya Infraventure Pvt. Ltd. has developed Saya Residency project. Available with
all facilities and amenities including Skydeck, Earth quake resistant, High Ceiling, Private Terrace/Garden, Located in the Heart of
city, 3 Tier Security System, Grand Entrance lobby, Premium branded fittings, Park, Lift, DEM Cygnus is an ideal option to enjoy a
contemporary lifestyle. With all modern conveniences at your disposal, Saya Residency will ensure a quality living experience.

Saya Residency Floor Plan

Floor plans are the perfect way to understand how the spaces/rooms of Saya Residency are structured and help provide a clear picture
of the house layout. The various units in Saya Residency come in 3 different configurations and have 6 types of layouts. 1 BHK Flat comes
in various sizes of 550 sq. ft super area. The uniquely designed 2 BHK Flat comes in a variety of floor plans of 722, 725, 750 and 800 sq. ft
super area.The aesthetically designed 3 BHK Flat is offered in attractive sizes like 1000 sq. ft super area. Bathrooms and balconies
are a part of these configurations. Aashiyana enumerates different configurations of Saya Residency across a range of unit sizes.
